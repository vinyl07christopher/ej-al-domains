Registration

  • For authors of accepted articles and participants, please complete the registration and payment according to the following methods.

  • At least one author of each accepted paper (preferably the corresponding author) must register and pay the fee before the conference registration deadline (20 December, 2023).

  • The registration fee for a paper (IEEE member and Non-IEEE member) is as given below, each paper is allowed to 6 pages, if you require to extend the page length, you can go up to 8 pages. The registration fee paid towards the conference covers attending the 3-day conference program, attending technical sessions, keynote or plenary talks, research discussions, lunch and dinner, refreshments, and conference kit. Traveling and lodging fees excluded.
