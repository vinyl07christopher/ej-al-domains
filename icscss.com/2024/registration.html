Registration

Each accepted work must have at least one author should be registered to ensure the inclusion of the article in the conference proceedings. Each registration will enable just one paper to be published and presented. 

Travel Grant

Travel grant will be afforded to the selected best (IEEE student members) research works

Required Final Registration Documents:

  • Final updated paper
  • Publishing agreement
  • Payment acknowledgement screenshot

Cancellation / Refund Policy

Refund/Cancellation is not possible after the registration.

Visa Information

ICSCSS will not directly contact embassies and consulates on behalf of visa applicants. All delegates should directly apply. However, to support the visa approval process, ICSCSS can provide an invitation letter to attend the conference.
